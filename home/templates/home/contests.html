Contests

National Anthem

Have you ever thought what it would be like to sing the national Anthem in front of 40,000 people and in front of millions on tv?

Well, here is your chance! Out at Wrigley will be holding a competition for one lucky and talented person to sing the national Anthem at Wrigley Field.

There will be two rounds. The first round is your song of choice and the second round will be the National Anthem.

There can be multiple winners at each prelim event that will advance to the semi-finals.

Semi Finals will be during Pridefest and Finals will be during Market Days. There will be a cash prize for 1st, 2nd and 3rd place. Visit Out at Wrigley Facebook, Twitter, or Instagram pages for more info on how to compete and the locations.

First Pitch

Win a chance to throw out the first pitch at Wrigley Field.

Contest Rules
  • There will be a round target with points.
  • Each contestant will be about 10 feet from the target.
  • Each contestant will have three throws per round.
  • There will be three rounds to throw at the target.
  • The highest scores each round will advance until there is a winner.

Semi Finals will be during Pridefest and Finals will be during Market Days. There will be a cash prize for 1st, 2nd and 3rd place. Visit Out at Wrigley Twitter, Instagram or Facebook Out at Wrigley Pages for more info on how to compete and the locations.
